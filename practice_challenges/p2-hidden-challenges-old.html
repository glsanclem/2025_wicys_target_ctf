    <p><strong>Methodology + Solution:</strong><br>

      This was another challenge that required reading the instructions carefully and paying close attention to details while analyzing the information. I immediately noticed the first letter in each sentence was in bold and highlighted. I knew something was off and kept that detail in mind as I continued to read through the instructions. Once finished, I was able to quickly go back and jot down each letter that I noticed earlier to obtain the flag. The lesson here is to follow your intuition. If something is off, make a take note of it as it just might be the answer to your flag.
