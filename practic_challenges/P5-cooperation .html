      In this challenge I learned that we were allowed to collaborate with others and ask for help. Collaboration allows us to help each other find the causes of the problem while also learning different angles. Giving helping hand may help the giver find joy when the receiver moves forward in the challenge. In turn, asking for help allows you to learn a different perspective and understand the challenges that you faced in getting the answer so the next time you are able to tackle the problem better. In the end, it’s a learning process and we are all winners!
